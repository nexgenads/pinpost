FAQ

Common questions

What platforms does Pinpost support?
Pinpost supports Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and Pinterest. Connect all five from a single dashboard with no switching required.
Can I manage multiple client accounts?
Yes. Pro plan supports up to 3 client brands, Agency plan supports up to 10. Each brand has its own calendar, team access, and analytics.
How does the AI campaign planner work?
You enter your client's product, target audience, campaign goal, and location. Pinpost AI recommends the best platforms, content types, and posting schedule for you.
Is there a free trial or money back guarantee?
Yes. You can start on the Free plan with no credit card. If you upgrade to Pro or Agency, we offer a 14-day money back guarantee — no questions asked.
Can my team members access client accounts?
Yes. Pro supports up to 10 team members per brand. Agency supports up to 25. Each member has their own login with brand-level access control.
How is Pinpost different from Hootsuite?
Pinpost is built specifically for agencies at half the price. We include AI campaign planning, multi-brand management, and team collaboration — all features that Hootsuite charges premium rates for.
