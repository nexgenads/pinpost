At Pinpost, operated by Appents LLC, we respect your right to control your personal data. This Deletion Policy explains what happens when you delete your account, what data is removed, and how you can request deletion.

01

Your right to deletion

You have the right to delete your Pinpost account and all associated personal data at any time, free of charge, with no questions asked. This right applies regardless of your subscription plan or account status.

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Once deletion is complete, your data cannot be recovered.

⚠️

Important: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Before deleting, we recommend downloading any content or data you wish to keep. We cannot recover deleted accounts or data after the deletion is processed.

02

What gets deleted

When you delete your Pinpost account, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your name, email address, and account credentials
  • All brand profiles and connected social media account tokens
  • All scheduled and drafted posts stored in Pinpost
  • All uploaded media files (images, videos) stored in Pinpost
  • All campaign data and AI campaign plans
  • All analytics data associated with your account
  • All team member records and collaboration data
  • All billing history and payment method references (actual card data is held by Stripe)
03

What is not deleted

Published social media posts

Posts that have already been published to Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest, or Google Business remain on those platforms. Pinpost does not have the ability to delete content from third-party platforms on your behalf. You must delete those posts directly from each platform.

Financial and legal records

We are required by law to retain certain billing and transaction records for up to 7 years for tax and financial compliance purposes. This data is held securely and is not used for any marketing purpose.

Anonymized analytics

We may retain anonymized, non-identifiable usage statistics that cannot be linked back to your account. These are used solely to improve our product.

04

How to delete your account

You can delete your account in two ways:

1

From your Pinpost dashboard

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Confirm your decision when prompted. Your account will be scheduled for deletion and permanently removed within 30 days.

2

Via email request

Email us at privacy@pinpost.io with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email.

05

Data deletion timeline

Day 0 — Request received

Your account is immediately deactivated. You can no longer log in or use Pinpost. Your subscription is cancelled (if active) with no further charges.

Within 7 days

All scheduled posts, campaigns, and media files are removed from our active systems. Connected social media tokens are revoked.

Within 30 days

All remaining personal data — including your profile, team records, and analytics — is permanently deleted from our databases and backups. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

06

Facebook data deletion

In compliance with Meta Platform Policy, Pinpost provides a dedicated Facebook Data Deletion callback URL. If you connected your Facebook account to Pinpost and wish to delete all associated data, you can:

  • Delete your Pinpost account using the steps above, or
  • Revoke Pinpost's access directly from your Facebook account at Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites → Pinpost → Remove
  • Email us at privacy@pinpost.io with the subject "Facebook Data Deletion Request"

Upon receiving a Facebook data deletion request, we will remove all data associated with your Facebook login within 30 days and provide a confirmation code for your records.
