We are Appents LLC ("Company", "we", "us", "our"), a company registered in the United States at 455 Market St Ste 1940, San Francisco, California 94105-2448. We operate the website pinpost.io and related services (the "Services").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use Pinpost. By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have questions, contact us at policy@pinpost.io.

01

What information we collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use our Services.

Information you provide

Name, email address, password, billing information, and any content you upload or create within Pinpost (posts, images, captions, campaign data).

Social media account data

When you connect a social media account (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Google Business, Pinterest), we receive access tokens and account metadata needed to schedule and publish your content.

Automatically collected data

IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for this purpose.

Payment information

Billing details are processed by our payment provider (Stripe). We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.

02

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Pinpost Services
  • Schedule and publish content to your connected social media accounts
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, account alerts, password resets)
  • Send product updates and marketing communications (you can opt out at any time)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve product features
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We process your information only when we have a valid legal basis to do so — including your consent, performance of a contract, our legitimate interests, or compliance with law.

03

When we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: We share data with trusted vendors who help us operate Pinpost (hosting, payments, analytics, email delivery). They are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Social media platforms: We share your content and credentials with the platforms you connect (Meta, TikTok, Google, Pinterest) solely to publish on your behalf.
  • Business transfers: If Pinpost is acquired or merges with another company, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Protection of rights: We may share data to protect the rights, property, or safety of Pinpost, our users, or the public.
04

Cookies and tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate our Services, remember your preferences, and understand how you use Pinpost.

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand usage and improve the product (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Marketing cookies: Used to show relevant ads on third-party platforms (e.g. Meta Pixel). You can opt out via your browser settings or cookie preferences.

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of Pinpost.

05

Data retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our Services. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or financial compliance purposes.

Content you have published to social media platforms through Pinpost remains on those platforms according to their own policies. Pinpost does not control third-party platform data retention.

06

Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing, including direct marketing.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at policy@pinpost.io. We will respond within 30 days.

07

Children's privacy

Pinpost is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at policy@pinpost.io and we will delete it promptly.

08

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites and platforms. This Privacy Policy applies only to Pinpost. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

09

Changes to this policy

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of Pinpost after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
